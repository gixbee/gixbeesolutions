What is Gixbee?

Gixbee is like a super-app for everyday services. Think of it like Uber + LinkedIn + Just Dial — all in one place.

🛍 Book Services

Need a plumber? A photographer for your wedding? A generator for an event? Book it here.

🎓 Find a Job

Fresh graduates and job-seekers apply for real jobs near them. Get interview calls directly on the app.

⚡ Earn by Working

Got a skill? List it. Go live. Get hired instantly for hourly gig work — like being an Uber driver, but for any skill.

🏢 List My Business

Own a catering company or hall? List it, manage bookings, add staff, and transfer ownership — all from the app.


🌟 The Golden Rule of Gixbee

Gixbee never assigns anyone to anyone. Every booking, every job acceptance, every hire — is a conscious choice made by the user. Gixbee is just the bridge. This is called the Safe Harbor principle.

✅ Customer chooses a vendor
✅ Worker chooses to accept
✅ Employer chooses who to hire

📊 Complete Instant Help Flow
1
Customer selects service + location
They pick the skill they need (Electrician, Driver, etc.) and specify where the service should happen — could be their home, parent's house, or office.
2
Presence Check — who is at the location?
The app asks: will YOU be there, or someone else? If someone else (e.g. elderly parent), the customer fills in their name, relation, and phone number. This is the On-Site Contact — the arrival OTP will be sent to THEM, not the customer.
🔑 This is critical — it ensures the OTP reaches the right person
3
System finds nearby active workers
Gixbee searches for all verified workers with that skill who are currently Live (active). Push notifications are sent to matching workers simultaneously. First to accept gets the job.
⏱ Workers have 90 seconds to accept before the request expires
4
Worker accepts → Customer confirms
Worker taps Accept. Customer sees the worker's profile and confirms. Background jobs start: 7-minute reminder and 10-minute GPS movement check.
5
Background monitoring begins
Every GPS ping from the worker's device is saved. At 7 minutes: worker gets a reminder push notification. At 10 minutes: if the worker hasn't moved, the booking is auto-cancelled and the worker gets a strike.
🚗 3 strikes = account suspended
6
Worker arrives — Arrival OTP
Worker taps "Arrived". The system sends a 4-digit OTP to the on-site contact (or customer). Worker must enter this OTP to officially start the job. Status changes to ACTIVE.
7
Worker finishes — Completion OTP
Worker taps "Finish". Another OTP is sent. Customer verifies the worker's OTP. Status changes to COMPLETED. Billing is calculated: minimum 1 hour, rounded up.
8
Payment + Rs.12 Deduction
Customer pays the worker. Rs.12 is deducted from the worker's Gixbee wallet as a platform fee. If this was the worker's FIRST ever job, they receive Rs.12 welcome bonus instead!
💰 First job: Free. After that: maintain Rs.12 balance to stay active.
🔄 Booking Status Flow
REQUESTED → ACCEPTED → ACTIVE → COMPLETED
Alternative paths: REJECTED (vendor said no) | CANCELLED (auto-cancel on GPS check fail)

Rental Service — Equipment for Events

Rent generators, mic sets, coolers, pressure washers — by the hour, for your event day.

🔑 Key Rules
1
Rental is inside Plan Services, NOT Instant Help
Because delivery logistics are involved — it needs planning.
2
Entire day is blocked on confirmation
Avoids return-delay conflicts. Generator booked for Nov 22 = whole day blocked.
3
Minimum hour billing
Use 3 hours on a 4-hour minimum? You pay for 4 hours. Use 6 hours? Pay 6 hours.
4
No OTP required
After confirmation, customer and vendor coordinate directly. Gixbee steps back.
💡 Example
Rahul is in Kottayam but needs a generator for a wedding in Kochi on the 29th.

He opens Gixbee → Book Services → Plan Services → Rental → Generator → Kochi.

He sees PowerPro Generator: ₹1000/hour, minimum 4 hours.

He checks availability — 29th is green (available).

He sends a request. Vendor approves. Rahul confirms.

The 29th turns red (blocked). They coordinate delivery via phone.

Earn by Working — Become a Gig Worker

Got a skill? Turn it into income. This is the worker side of the Live Worker Engine.

📊 Worker Registration & Go-Live Flow
1
Register your skills
Choose from the master skill list: Electrician, Driver, Cleaner, Nurse, Carpenter, AC Technician, etc. Each skill needs document proof (certificate, ID, etc.).
2
Admin verification
Your submission goes to the Gixbee admin panel. Admin reviews your proof and approves or rejects. Status: PENDING → VERIFIED
Only verified workers appear on the customer's radar
3
Set your hourly rate
You decide how much you charge per hour (within the system range). You can update this maximum 2 times per day.
4
Set your availability schedule
Tell Gixbee when you're available: Monday 9am-5pm, Tuesday 3pm-7pm, etc. Customers can only find you during these hours.
5
Toggle Go-Live ⚡
Toggle the Go-Live switch ON. Your profile appears on the customer's live radar. Customers nearby can now send you job requests.
💰 First job: No wallet needed. After first job: must keep Rs.12 in wallet to go live again.
6
Accept jobs, complete, earn
When a customer picks your skill, you get a push notification. You have 90 seconds to accept. Complete the job using the OTP flow. Get paid directly by the customer.
💳
Rs.12 Wallet
Platform fee per job. After first job, must keep Rs.12 to stay live.
⚠️
Strike System
Accept a job but don't move toward customer = Strike. 3 strikes = Account suspended.
📊
Rate Limit
Hourly rate can be updated maximum 2 times per day. Resets at midnight.

📊 Business Registration & Management Flow
1
Choose business type and register
Select: Service / Hiring / Rental. Fill in business name, location, description. Upload legal proof (GST certificate, etc.). Status: PENDING
2
Admin reviews and verifies
Gixbee admin checks the documents and approves. Status: VERIFIED ✓. You get a push notification.
3
Business Unit Dashboard
You see a card for each of your verified businesses. Click a card to manage it: calendar, operators, offline days, bookings.
🃏 Card 1 → Royal Catering  |  🃏 Card 2 → City Caterers  |  ➕ Add New
4
Add operators (managers / HR)
Add a trusted person as an operator. They can manage bookings and calendars. They CANNOT transfer ownership. You remain in full control.
5
Ownership Transfer (if selling the business)
Initiate a transfer to the new owner. Both you AND the new owner must confirm with OTP. A 24-hour hold is applied before the transfer becomes final — giving time to cancel if it was a mistake.

💰 How the Wallet Works
New user joins: Gets Rs.100 starting balance
First gig job completed: Gets Rs.12 bonus
Every job accepted: Rs.12 deducted from wallet

🔐 The Rs.12 Rule
Think of it like a taxi driver needing to keep fuel in their car before they start a shift. Workers need Rs.12 "activation fuel" to be available on the Gixbee network. First time is free — after that, it's your responsibility to top up.

⚙️ How the Radar Works (Behind the Scenes)
1. Worker goes Live → their app starts sending GPS coordinates to the server via WebSocket (a live connection, not a regular refresh)
2. Server stores the GPS coordinates in Redis (super-fast memory database)
3. Server sends location update only to the customer tracking that specific worker's job
4. Customer's radar screen updates in real-time — the worker marker moves as they travel
5. If a worker stops moving for 5+ minutes → the 10-minute GPS check detects no movement → auto-cancel

What Powers Gixbee? (Simple Version)

You don't need to be a developer to understand this. Think of these as departments in a company.

🗄️ PostgreSQL Database

The main filing cabinet. Stores all users, bookings, jobs, businesses, wallet transactions — permanently.

⚡ Redis

Super-fast temporary memory. Stores OTP codes (for 5 minutes) and worker GPS locations. Data disappears after a set time — perfect for short-lived info.

🔔 Firebase FCM

The notification delivery system. When a booking is accepted, a job alert triggers, or a worker arrives — FCM pushes it to your phone instantly.

🔌 WebSocket

A live permanent connection between the app and server. Used for real-time GPS tracking. Like a phone call — always open, data flows both ways.

⏰ Bull Queue

A scheduled task manager. Fires the 7-minute reminder and 10-minute GPS check automatically after a booking is created — like a kitchen timer.

💳 Razorpay

India's payment gateway. Handles UPI, GPay, cards, net banking. Used to top up the Gixbee wallet.
